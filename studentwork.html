Student Work

Projects Made by Students

School Club

The School Club project was a advertisment designed by a student. The objective is to promote a school club with an animal head and human body.

Artist Genre Switch

For this project a student took an artist, Marshmellow, and created promo to advertise a new album and tour. Part of the project was to also change the genre of music.

Get Buzzed

Objective for this project is to create a advertisment for a food product. The goal is to reach a different audience.

HowardTravel Promo

Objective for students was to create promo for HowardTravel. A student used food to add to iconic landmarks.

Cheez Whiz

The Cheez Whiz project was to give a new redesign for this product. It was made to give a new brand look.

Hershey's Mona Lisa

A student created a Hershey's advertisment. The student used famous paintings by making it look like paintings were enjoying the chocolate.
