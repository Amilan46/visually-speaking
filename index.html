About Us

Vizually Speaking was made to show the programs offered in the Visual Communications program. The website showcases projects made by students in the program. It is meant to be an educational tool used by students in the Visual Communications program. Vizually Speaking is managed by students.

This website is used for educational purposes only for students in the Visual Communication program at LSC-CyFair. For all official information regarding Lone Star College, please visit www.lonestar.edu
